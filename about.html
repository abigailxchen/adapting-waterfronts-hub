Adapting Waterfronts is a global climate education initiative around sea level rise. We invite the public to explore the past, present, and potential future transformations of the shorelines of cities across the world as they adapt to climate change.

Supported by funds from the Research, Scholarly and Creative Activities (RCSA) Grant Program at the California Polytechnic State University in San Luis Obispo.
